Sentara — Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 17, 2025

Overview

Sentara (“the App”) helps you track emotions, reflect, and receive AI-assisted insights. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account & Subscription Data: email, purchase tokens, and entitlements from app stores (e.g., Google Play/RevenueCat) to manage your subscription.
  • App Activity: check-ins, reflections, mood history, and feature usage you choose to save on your device or sync.
  • Device Data: basic device and app diagnostics (e.g., app version, OS version, crash logs) for reliability and abuse prevention.

We do not sell personal data.

How We Use Data

  • Provide core features (mood tracking, reflections, AI insights) and personalize your experience.
  • Manage purchases and subscriptions.
  • Improve app stability, performance, and safety.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

AI & Cloud Processing

To generate insights, the App may securely send your prompts and relevant context to AI services. We minimize the data sent and use encryption in transit. Consult our in-app settings to control what’s shared.

Data Storage & Retention

  • On-Device: Many entries (like mood history) are stored locally unless you choose to back up or sync.
  • Cloud: If you enable features requiring cloud services (e.g., subscriptions, notifications), related data is stored as needed.
  • Retention: We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law.

Sharing

We share data with vendors only to operate the App (e.g., payments, analytics, crash reporting, AI processing). These providers are bound by confidentiality and data-protection obligations. We may disclose information if required by law, to protect rights, or to prevent fraud or abuse.

Your Choices & Controls

  • Adjust privacy and data settings in the App.
  • Request access, correction, export, or deletion by emailing support@yourdomain.com.
  • Opt-out of optional analytics if offered in settings.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe we’ve collected data from a child, contact us to request deletion.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we continually improve our protections.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date and, when appropriate, notify you in-app.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email support@yourdomain.com.
